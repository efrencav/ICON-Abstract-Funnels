Common Questions

Everything You Need to Decide

What's the difference between the Accelerator and buying Transformation + Growth Partnership separately?
Beyond the ~$5,000 in savings, the structural difference is continuity. When you buy separately, there's an onboarding gap between the build team and the marketing team. The Accelerator uses the same team throughout—which means no re-briefing, no positioning drift, and no momentum loss. The Growth Phase activates directly from the build, using the same messaging architecture, same identity system, and the same strategic foundation.
How is payment structured for the full Accelerator?
The Build Phase uses the standard 30/30/40 structure: 30% at contract signing, 30% at strategy presentation, 40% at final delivery. The Growth Phase is billed monthly, beginning with Month 1 execution. Total investment is $39,000–$44,000 depending on scope. All payment structures are locked in the agreement before work begins.
What happens at the end of the 6-month Growth Phase?
At month six, you receive a full growth review session and a prioritized 12-month roadmap. From there, you can continue on the standard Growth Partnership retainer ($3,000–$3,500/month), take the system in-house using the brand guidelines and documented processes, or pause. The system is fully documented so no knowledge is lost.
Can I start with just the Complete Transformation and add Growth later?
Yes. The Complete Transformation is $20,000–$25,000 and includes a 30-day optimization window. After that, you can start the Growth Partnership retainer at $3,000–$3,500/month. However, the Accelerator pricing ($39,000–$44,000) saves approximately $5,000 versus purchasing in sequence—and eliminates the onboarding gap. If you know you'll want ongoing execution, committing at the start is the better investment.
How much executive time is required during the 8-month engagement?
The Build Phase requires executive participation—approximately 4–6 hours per week during strategy and identity phases, tapering to 2–3 hours during the website and funnel phases. The Growth Phase requires monthly review meetings (60–90 minutes) and availability to approve content direction. We structure around executive calendars and give clear decision windows so reviews don't become bottlenecks.
