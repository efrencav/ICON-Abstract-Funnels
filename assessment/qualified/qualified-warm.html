Why Strategic Fit Matters

Our best clients have three things in common: (1) executive sponsorship locked before we start, (2) willingness to address positioning before execution, and (3) commitment to implementing recommendations. If any of these are missing, we'll recommend alternative partners or resources.

Pro tip: Bring your CMO, Marketing Director, or Sales VP to the call if they'll be implementing the work. We'll address their concerns and align on sequencing together.

No-pressure guarantee: If we're not the right fit, we'll tell you on the call—and recommend better alternatives. We turn down 40% of qualified leads because fit matters more than revenue.

Common Questions from Qualified Leads

What if I'm not sure we're ready yet?

Book the call anyway. We'll help you assess readiness and identify gaps (budget approval, executive alignment, timeline constraints). If you're not ready, we'll recommend a waiting period and provide resources to close the gaps.

Do we need our CEO/Owner on the first call?

Not required, but helpful if they're the final decision-maker. If you're a CMO/Director with budget authority, that's sufficient—but we'll ask about executive sponsorship and may request a follow-up with leadership if needed.

How much does a typical engagement cost?

Our Strategic Foundation starts at $35K (positioning, brand strategy, messaging architecture). Most clients then move into an ongoing partnership ($8K-$15K/month) for brand identity, infrastructure, and growth support. We'll discuss investment on the call once we confirm scope.
