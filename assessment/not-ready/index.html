When to Request a Strategic Fit Call

Once you've addressed the gaps identified in your assessment, you'll be ready for a productive conversation. Here's what needs to be in place:

Decision Authority Confirmed

A clear decision owner (CEO, Owner, CMO with budget authority) is identified and committed to attending the call. "Decision by committee" won't work.

Budget Allocated

Minimum $10K–$12K for Brand Foundation (positioning, brand strategy, messaging). Ideally $20K–$25K for Complete Transformation, or $39K–$44K for the Accelerator bundle (includes 6 months of partnership).

Executive Participation Available

Leadership team (CEO, CMO, VP Sales) can commit to attending positioning workshop and quarterly reviews. Strategic work requires executive input.

Priorities Aligned Internally

Marketing, sales, and leadership agree on the constraint (positioning vs. execution) and the sequence (strategy first, then infrastructure).
