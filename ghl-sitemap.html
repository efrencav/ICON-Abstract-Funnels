GHL Setup Checklist

Step 1 — Custom Fields
Create in GHL → Contacts
□ Qualification Tier (Dropdown: Hot / Warm / Lukewarm / Not Ready)
□ Lead Score (Number)
□ Assessment Completed (Checkbox)
□ Diagnostic Downloaded (Checkbox)
□ Company Revenue Range (Dropdown)
□ Decision Authority (Dropdown)
□ Engagement Stage (Text)
Step 2 — Form Field Mapping
Map assessment answers → custom fields
□ Assessment result → Qualification Tier field
□ Company name → Company field
□ Phone → Phone (with SMS opt-in copy)
□ Revenue range → Custom field
□ Decision authority → Custom field
□ Map calendar source per tier (Hot / Warm / Lukewarm)
Step 3 — Workflow Goal Conditions
Add to every active sequence
□ Automation 1: Stop when assessment-complete added
□ Automations 3/4/5: Stop when fit-call-booked added
□ Automation 7: Stop when appointment marked complete or no-show
□ Nurture: Stop when contact re-enters active sequence
□ All sequences: Stop if tagged client
