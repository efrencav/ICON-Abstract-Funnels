How It Works

Access Is Set Up Before Your Project Kicks Off

The portal is exclusive to active Abstract Creative clients. You'll set up your password when you're onboarded — from there, your workspace is ready on Day 1.

1
Proposal signed & deposit received

Your project is confirmed and your portal workspace is created in the system with your project details pre-loaded.

2
You set up your password & log in

You'll receive an invitation email with a link to create your password. Once set, you can log in anytime at portal.abstract-creative.com.

3
Complete your intake in the portal

Your onboarding questionnaire, brand intake, and kickoff scheduling all happen inside the portal — keeping everything in one place from day one.

4
Track everything through delivery

From first strategy session to final asset delivery, every update, approval, file, and invoice lives in your portal — no chasing status emails.
